# Incident 001 - Password Reset

## Ticket Information

**Ticket ID:** INC-001

**Priority:** Low

**Status:** Closed

**Assigned Team:** Tier 1 Help Desk

---

## User Reported Issue

The user was unable to access company resources after forgetting their account password.

---

## Troubleshooting Process

1. Verified the user's identity.
2. Checked account status in Active Directory.
3. Confirmed account was not locked.
4. Reset the user's password.
5. Enabled "User must change password at next logon."

---

## Root Cause

User forgot their password after an extended period away from the system.

---

## Resolution

Password was reset successfully and access to company resources was restored.

---

## Lessons Learned

Recommend implementing self-service password reset capabilities to reduce help desk workload.

---

Response Time: 10 Minutes
